War Diary

 

384th Field Battery RA 142nd Field Regiment RA

 

PLACE

DATE

 

 

Northiam

Aug 1 1940

Don force exercise. 384th Bty attacked 275 Anti-tank Bty who were defending Mountfield Park. The attack was successful. A fine day.

 

ADS

 

2

A Brigade signal exercise in the Ninfield area. 384 Bty was in support of the 8th Devons. Don Tp shot on the open range. A fine day.

 

ADS

 

3

Troop training and inspections in the morning. Half holiday in the afternoon. A fine day.

 

ADS

 

4

Church Parade at the Parish Church, Preacher the Rev Dunbar. A fine day. Capt Chamberlain left for a three weeks course at Larkhill. Lt. Johnson returned to Don Tp.

 

ADS

 

5

A Divisional exercise was held. Don force stood to from 0330 to 0530 and were called out to occupy the Rye line at 0950 hrs. A hot day.

 

ADS

 

6

HQ shot on Battle range. Don Troop did gun Drill on 25 pdrs at Rye. 4 French 75mm guns arrived at 0700 hrs. Captain Stoddart gave a lecture on them in the evening, having been to a course at Larkhill. 4 gun tractors arrived in the afternoon. A fine day.

 

ADS

 

7

Officers of the Battery proceeded to Artillery ranges at Westdean to watch the 96th Fd Regiment RDYA shoot. The shooting was good and most instructive. E Tp did 25 Pdr gun drill. A fine day.

 

CC

 

8

Don Tp 75mm gun drill. E Tp shot on the open range at Battle. A fine day. Acs and NCOs went to watch shooting at Westdean.

 

CC

 

9

A Don force exercise was held in the Robertsbridge area. The enemy were represented by the 8th Devons. Don force arrived at the objective first but were driven off. A counter attack was launched and the enemy were driven from the objective. The two armoured cars which arrived the night before were taken out. A fine day.

 

CC

 

10

E Tp shot on the open ranges at Westdean. The gun drill was good. Officers of the Battery went to watch the shooting. A fine day.

 

CC

 

11

Church Parade at the Parish Church. Preacher the Rev Dunbar. A fine day.

 

CC

 

12

Officers went to see 383 Bty shoot on the ranges at Westdean. Reconnoitred a troop position in the Pett area for the 4-5 hours. Bombs were dropped in the Rye area. One very heavy one near the Winchelsea OP. A sunny day. Captain Boileau proceeded on leave.

 

CC

 

13

Don and Freddie Troops shot on the ranges. The shooting and gun drill was good. A fine day.

 

CC

 

14

Reconnoitred Troop position in the Pett area. Four 75mm (British) guns were fetched from Rye. The four 75mm guns (French) were taken down to Rye from Northiam for the static position. A hot fine day.

 

CC

 

15

Don force exercise. The whole force was called out. A fine day.

 

CC

 

16

Don force exercise. The last to be held. Don force having now been disbanded. A fine day.

 

CC

 

17

Don Tp gun drill on the French 75mm guns. Half holiday in the afternoon. A fine day.

 

CC

 

18

Church Parade at the Parish Church 1000 hrs. A fine day.

 

CC

 

19

Don Tp and F Troop proceeded to the Eastbourne ranges to shoot the French 75mm guns. A successful shoot. 2/Lt Roland Jones proceeded on leave. A fine day.

 

CC

 

20

Don Tp did a drill order on river Rother and Isle of Oxney. A fine day. E Tp were working on new position at Pett.

 

CC

 

21

E Tp did a drill order on River Rother & Isle of Oxney. A fine day.

 

CC

 

22

F Troop did an exercise under command of the RIF at Pevensey. Remainder did Lewis gun instruction. A fine day.

 

CC

 

23

The Battery did a night move and Bivouac exercise in Brickwall Park. The movement ran to schedule in spite of it being a very dark night. A fine day.

 

CC

 

24

Troop training. A fine day.

 

CC

 

25

Church Parade at the Parish Church Northiam. A fine day.

 

CC

 

26

E Tp moved into position at Rye. C Tp returned to Northiam. Lewis gun instruction. A fine day.

 

CC

 

27

Troop training. A fine day.

 

CC

 

28

D & F Troops did an exercise under command of the 6th Devons in the Battle area. A fine day.

 

CC

 

29

Troop training. Lewis gun instruction. A fine day.

 

CC

 

30

Troop training. A fine day.

 

CC

 

31

F Troop did an exercise under command of the RIF on the Romney Marsh. A fine day.
