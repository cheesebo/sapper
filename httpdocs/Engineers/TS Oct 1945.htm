War Diary

 

158th Railway Construction Company Royal Engineers

 

PLACE

DATE

 

 

 

Leer

1 Oct 45

 

OC met CO at Bridge No 153 Elbergen. All piling completed. Hope to have br ready for traffic on 3 October. Instr Lt Macdonald to impose speed restriction of 10 Kmph and to have German flagmen in attendance.

 

 

2

 

OC to Leda Br with 2 i/c. VD film shown to all ranks at Leer

 

 

 

3

 

Br No 153 Elbergen opened for traffic. Track still has to be levelled and realigned and surplus materials loaded on to rail for despatch. HQ played football match against Lingen detachment.

Capt Pollock to Paris on 72 hrs leave

 

 

 

4

 

OC to Leda Br. 1 pile and bracing in pier No 2 damaged by German Constructor working on old br. Pile repaired and bracing replaced. Work remaining to be done – fix guard rails on deck spans, drive drift pins and weld guide strips for girders.

 

 

 

5

 

OC to Br No 153 Elbergen and to Leda Br Leer

OC inf by A/D Tn , BACR, he will not be retained but released with his A&S Gp in the normal way.

 

 

 

6

 

OC to Leda Br.

 

 

 

9

 

Capt Pollock reported back from Paris leave

 

 

 

10

 

OC to Lingen detachment and Br No 153 Elbergen. Arranged for Lt MacDonald to be att 926 Rly Br Coy on 11 October, pending posting.  DAD Tn, 30 Corps phoned to say Unit disbandment order received at 30 corps and sent to unit through normal channels.

 

 

 

11

 

Lt Jayne reported back from UK leave

 

 

 

12

 

OC visited 3 Cdn Inf Div (CAOF) re unit disbandment. Saw DAQMG (Br) and obtained copy of disbandment order which he informed me will reach me officially through 301 Inf Bde.

 

 

 

13

 

‘Phoned CO. Objected to his posting away 2 clerks as they cannot be spared with disbandment imminent. Had posting order reduced to 1 clerk only. Played football match against Leer Town. Lost 4-1.

 

 

 

14

 

Received Instrs from CO to hold on to a few tools for maintenance work and not return until the last minute.

 

 

 

15

 

The CO has now posted away my only NCO fitter IC and pumps. Wrote protesting against posting of key men.

 

 

 

16

 

Train of surplus plant and materials from Brs Nos 153 and 156 left Leer for 102 TnSD, Hamburg

 

 

 

17

 

Received order for unit disbandment from 301 Inf Bde.

Completed reconstruction and maintenance of Br No 153, Elbergen.

 

 

 

18

 

Lingen detachment returned to Leer.

 

 

 

19

 

2000

OC saw CO at Group HQ. Discussed final arrangements for unit disbandment.

Received bulk posting orders from the D Tn for all ORs except 3 clerks.

 

 

 

20

 

Lt Jayne called on ADOS 3 Cdn Inf Div (CAOF) re instrs for disposal of our MT. ADOS unable to find the list I sent in a week ago. Lt Jayne to call again on 22 October.

 

 

 

21

 

Posted 79 ORs to 151, 152, 603 and 607 Rly Constr Coys. No more works can be carried out now.

 

 

 

22

 

Br No 153 at Elbergen and No 156 at Leer handed over to RBD for maintenance; thus this unit no longer responsible for any works.

 

 

 

24

2000

Received instrs from CO to hold up postings to 607 Rly Constr Coy, and to send off to 603 Rly Constr Coy all postings in A&S Gps 32 and over.

Personnel in A&S Gps earlier than 32 to be retained pending further instrs.

 

 

 

26

 

Capt Pollock to Hannover to attend interview for Mil Gov apt

 

 

 

28

 

CO agreed in telephone conversation with OC that OC can leave Unit for release when PRI funds and Imprest a/c closed and audited. Copy of letter to CO showing state of Unit disbandment is attached.

 

 

 

29

2359

Unit transferred for local Admin from HQ 301 Inf Bde to HQ 31 A A Bde. Unit Imprest a/c closed and handed in to 18 CPO by Capt Bamford.

 

 

 

30

 

Audit Board convened by OC for final audit of PRI, Offr’s & Sgt’s Messes accounts. DAD Tn, 30th Corps called Coy HQ.

Tele:- via 2/7 Cdn RECCE Regt                                   Confidential

 

Subject:- Disbandment of Units

          Postings – ORs

 

To:- CO

     No 6 Rly Constr & Maint Gp RE

 

Reference your 6CM/2127/752 dated 14 Oct 45

 

Whereas I have no wish to prejudice A/Cpl Cave’s promotion I am much more concerned that the unit transport be kept on the road and handed back to Ordnance in good condition. I merely requested that the posting be held over for two (2) weeks and did not anticipate it would have to be cancelled.

 

L/Sgt Roney LT is in the driver class; Cpl Cave is a fitter IC & pumps, so theie jobs are not interchangeable: my only other reliable fitter was released last week.

 

As bulk posting of all personnel is not going to be ordered until about two (2) weeks time, I request that in future key personnel are not posted while I still hold the bulk of my G.1098 and full scale of transport, otherwise I submit I cannot be held responsible for the efficiency of the unit disbandment.

 

A/Cpl Cave will be dispatched forthwith.

 

15th Oct 45                                   RT Jones   Major RE

Leer Nr Emden                            OC 158 Rly Const Coy RE

Disbandment progress is as follows:-

Account closed and Unit now attached to 172 Coy PC for pay.

III.   PRI

Account will be closed and audited on 30 Oct 45. 172 Coy PC are arranging to draw for week commencing 4 Nov 1945

IV.     MT

Now held M/Cs – 2

         Cars – 5     Can 2 Lorries be attached from 603 Coy (say)

   Lorries 3T – 9     on 31 Oct to enable the return of vehicles to

          W/T – 1     RVP Hamburg to be completed please?

More vehicles will be returned on 30 Oct as numbers of drivers available permit.

V.      G.1098

With the exception of cooking gear, arms and ammunition and several small items all G.1098 has now been returned.

VI.     Office machinery

Still held

VII.   Clothing

Spare clothing will be vouchered over to 603 Coy this week.

VIII. Sports and Welfare Kit

Vouchered over to 603 and 607 Coys

IX.     Disbandment Date

The posting away of ORs has been somewhat delayed by the non-arrival of personnel from UK leave owing to sailing cancellations and the cancellation of postings to 603 Coy, but the final disbandment should be completed by 10 Nov 1945 at the latest.

 

Leer                                RT Jones      Major RE
