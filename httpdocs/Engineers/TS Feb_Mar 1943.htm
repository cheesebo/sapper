War Diary

 

158th Railway Construction Company Royal Engineers

 

PLACE

DATE

 

Tn TC “Weston Camp” Derby

Feb 1st 1943 to Feb 28th

Company employed on bridging course during whole of month. 2158073 Spr Ollard EHA died WOAS Weston Camp 1100 hrs Feb 1st

 

 

Feb 26th

Company squad broke both decontamination & repair records in “contaminated crater scheme”

 

Feb 26th

Company moved to “Woolmer Camp” Tn TC Longmoor Hants.

 

“Woolmer Camp” Tn TC Longmoor Hants

Feb 28th to Mar 13th

Company on military training

 

Mar 1st

Major RGM Matthews visited war office Tn 4b

 

 

Mar 3rd

Received mobilisation instructions 79/MOB/5774/21 (AG 5 MOB) to be completed by 14th Mar 1943

 

 

Mar 14th

Detachments moved to Morfa Rd Camp Harlech & Moreton Court, Moreton on Lugg

 

 

Mar 15th

Company HQ moved to “Admington Hall” Shipston-on-Stour, Warwickshire & detachment to “Adlestrop camp” nr Kingham.

 

 

Mar 15th to Mar 31st

Company employed on railway construction work for RCE (Western) & RCE (Southern)

 

 

RGM Matthews
