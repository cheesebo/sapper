384th Battery spend this time in ground warfare training and taking part in invasion exercises. They move first to Tickhill, near Doncaster before heading back South to Basildon Park and then on to Essex.

A short illness takes John to Charlton Park, Malmesbury, converted to a military hospital; near enough to Hankerton for his wife to be able to cycle over and visit him.
