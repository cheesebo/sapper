0915-1000

Foot & Boot Inspection. Boots were found to be in a bad state. Feet were on the whole in good condition.

Gun Drill. NCOs Guard Duty. Games in the afternoon. Very wet day.

1400

Gunners lecture Night Occupation and PT remainder. PT and night occupation. Duty men paraded with 384th Bty for baths at Okehampton Camp.

0915-1000

Battery marching drill in morning.

Specialists and Signallers had a lay out in the morning.

 

 

14.10.39

 

Full kit inspection was held in the morning. Kit was found to be complete and in good order.

Church Parade at Okehampton Parish Church.

Battery marching drill

17.10.39

0915-1000

Battery marching drill on football field.

 

 

 

1030-1230

Gun Drill. Drivers, specialists & signallers under their own Instructors. Bath parade in the afternoon. Three men for reserved occupations were discharged. No 921702 Gnr Endicott, No 917176 L/Bdr Rottenburg, No 852427 Gunner Hosegood

 

 

18.10.39

 

Foot & Boot Inspection in the morning. Boots were in a bad state feet were good. Signalling Sergeants, Troop SMs and Officers had motor cycle instruction.

 

 

19.10.39

0900-1230

Battery Staff Drill Order on Dartmoor. Very thick mist during the early part of the exercise.

 

 

 

1345-1415

Lecture on points from the drill order. An order was given that all radiators must be emptied at night.

 

 

20.10.39

0915-1230

Route march along Hatherleigh road.

Pay parade in afternoon. Very bright day. 29 militia then arrived.

 

 

21.10.39

0915-1000

Battery marching drill.

 

 

 

1030-1230

Public kit inspection. Water bottles were cleaned out with hot water & soda. A branch of the NAAFI was opened in the White Hart Garage.

 

 

22.10.39

 

Church Parade at the Parish Church Okehampton. Preacher the Rev Compton.

 

 

23.10.39

1515

Lecture on points from Drill Order.

1515
