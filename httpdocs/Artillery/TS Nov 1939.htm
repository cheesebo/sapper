Okehampton

Nov 2 1939

0900

Marching Drill

 

 

 

1015-1230

Gunners aiming practice. Drivers, Signallers & specialists under their own instructors. In future men were granted leave monthly from Friday 0900 hrs to Monday 2230 hrs.

 

 

 

3

0900

Route march in Battle order. March 10 miles to Jacobstowe and back. Wet day.

 

 

 

1400

Gunners cleaning guns, remainder PT.

 

 

 

4

0900

Battery marching drill. Kit & billet inspection. Half holiday in the afternoon.

 

 

 

5

0930

Church Parade. There was a roll call for all men not on leave.

 

 

 

6

0900

Battery marching drill. Gunners tactical exercise in South moor area. 20 Immatures left the Battery.  In the afternoon there was a lecture on the tactical exercise. A fine day.
