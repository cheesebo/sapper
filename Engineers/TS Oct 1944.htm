War Diary

 

158th Railway Construction Company Royal Engineers

 

PLACE

DATE

 

 

 

Lisieux

3 Oct 44

 

Outbreak of Diphtheria reported at Bernay – Town placed out of bounds

 

 

7

1200

Section No 5 moved from Moult to Caen – Accommodation in railway wagons on Troan branch line

 

 

 

1230

Major Millard and Lieut Bamford interview M. Naud, Chef de Section Caen SNCF – Taking over of maintenance by French discussed.

 

 

 

8

1800

Visit by Major Hawkins, OC 152 Rly Constr Coy RE, with instructions from RCE 6 that half his company & half 232 Pioneer Coy are to report to Lisieux to carry out additional work requd by Operating Coy.

 

 

 

9

1000

Half of 152 Rly Constr Coy RE arrive Lisieux and accommodated in 158 Rly Constr Coy billets. Additional operating facilities Lisieux  Station and Yards discussed on site by Lt Col Wootton, CO No 1 Canadian Rly Optg Group, Major Butland A/RCE 6, Major Alcorn OC No 1 Canadian Rly Optg Coy, Major Hawkins OC 152 Rly Constr Coy RE and Maj Millard OC 158 Rly Constr Coy RE.

 

 

 

10

1500

Half 232 Pioneer Coy arrive Lisieux to assist 152 Rly Constr Coy with work on station.

 

 

10

1745

M Layalley, Deputy Engineer SNCF Caen District, visited this HQ and agreed that he would be prepared to take over maintenance of track from Caen to Serquigny forthwith subject to the provision of tools by British Authorities – OC 158th Rly Constr Coy to take up with Higher Command.

 

 

 

12

1300

Inspection of line 3 from Serquigny to Lisieux by Major-Gen McMullen D Tn War Office, Lt Cols Scott RCE 6, Wootton OC No 1 Canadian Rly Optg Gp, Rees AD Tn Caen, Majors Butland A/RCE6, Millard OC 158 Rly Constr Coy and Capt Roulstone Adj RCE6

 

 

 

14

1800

Phase 1 of new operating facilities Lisieux Station completed

 

 

 

15

1100

Half 152 Rly Constr Coy leave Lisieux for new location

 

 

 

16

1100

Half 232 Pioneer Coy leave Lisieux for new location

 

 

 

17

1230

Works inspection visit by Major Butland and Capt Roulstone Lisieux Station

 

 

 

19

0800

232 Pioneer Coy leave works for new location

 

 

 

21

 

Inspection of works Lisieux by Brig Woodhouse DD Tn (Rly) War Office, Lt Col Everall and Major Beckett, Lt Col Scott RCE6

 

 

 

23

1500

Visit by Capts Hoar and Parsons Staff Capts 707 Rly Grand Division US Forces regarding hand-over from Caen (incl) to Lisieux (Excl)

 

 

 

24

0900

Section 5 disbanded – personnel return to their former sections

 

 

 

0900

Section 4 move from Mezidon to Coy HQ

 

 

 

1200

Major Millard visits Lt Col Freeman OC 720 Rly Optg Battn US Forces at Caen, to discuss hand-over of maintenance of track Caen – Lisieux

 

 

 

25

1000

Hand-over of line W3 Caen – Lisieux by Major Millard to Capt Barr 720 Rly Optg Battn US Forces

 

 

 

26

1600

Works inspection visit by Major Butland A/RCE6 Lisieux Station

 

 

 

0900

Section 3 move from Mesnil Mauger to Coy HQ

 

 

 

27

1300

Major Millard visits RCE6 at Elbeuf
