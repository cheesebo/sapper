War Diary

 

158th Railway Construction Company Royal Engineers

 

PLACE

DATE

 

 

 

Marbeck-Heiden

1 Jun 1945

0800

Advance party of HQ & Section 3 move to new location at Leer Nr Emden. MR Q805152 1/100,000 sheet M2.

 

 

2

0800

HQ Office & clerks move to new location at Leer

 

 

 

 

2100

Main party of No 2 Belgian Rly Constr Coy arrived to take over from all sections of 158 Coy and set up their HQ at Coesfeld.

 

 

 

3

0900

Main HQ party, Sections 1 & 4 move to new location at Leer.

 

 

Leer

4

 

Capt Sievwright 2 i/c 158 Rly Constr Coy recce line from Leer to Emden

 

 

 

 

Company responsibility for repair & reconstruction from Papenburg (incl Station) to Emden South (incl). German engineers found to be constructing two destroyed bridges at Oldersum. Bridge over river Leda approximately 719ft long completely destroyed and to be repaired by 158 Coy.

 

 

 

5

 

Work commenced on section Leda bridge, Leer to Oldersum.

 

 

 

1000

Major Millard OC 158 Rly Con Coy proceeded on UK leave, Capt Sievwright assumed command of the unit.

 

 

 

6

 

D Day anniversary GOC in C declared a general day off for all ranks.

 

 

 

11

 

Work commenced on clearing wreckage and dismantling Leda bridge (7 spans)

 

 

 

12

 

Capt Sievwright proceed on UK leave. Capt Pollock assumed command of the unit.

 

 

 

18

 

Major Millard returned from UK leave and assumed command. Inspected No 2 section at Holtwick MR 598760 1/100,000 sheet P1.

 

 

 

19

 

Major Millard reported to RCE6, Lt Col Scott at Darfeld and arrived Company HQ at Leer at 1800 hrs.

 

 

 

21

0900

Move of Coy HQ to new billets in Leer at MR 818148 1/100,000 Sheet M2. Section 1, 3 & 4 to remain at Leer MR 805152.

 

 

 

1600

Major Tredininck A/RCE visited works nothing to report.

 

 

 

24

1800

Section 2 move from Holtwick to Coy HQ at Leer

 

 

 

27

1600

Lt Hammond arrives at Coy HQ with rear party of Section 2.

 

 

 

 

FJ Millard
